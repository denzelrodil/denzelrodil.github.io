GOLDEN TICKET

"We are the Golden Ticket your Golden Years"

INTRODUCTION: Everyone likes paying less to do more, especially seniors who have worked their entire lives to build up savings. When seniors retire, they have a lot of time on their hand and no sustainable source of income. Golden Ticket helps seniors with obtaining discounts for restaurants, shopping, and other services offered through the application. We wanted to provide an intuitive interface that seniors and everyone else would enjoy using. Using this mobile app, seniors will be able to find fun activities to occupy their time while being financially responsible.

CHALLENGES: We analyzed other mobile apps to understand what features and designs we wanted to implement for Golden Ticket. We decided that we wanted to have a simple design with functional features such as a search bar and a map function for personalized suggestions. An easy-to-use interface was one of our main priorities because we wanted users to enjoy using the app as they searched for discounts. Deciding on this led us to create a way for users to find activities. We believed that organizing deals into a set of categories would help seniors find activities to do incase they weren't sure what they wanted.
