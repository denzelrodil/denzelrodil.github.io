DIGITAL LEARNING PLATFORM

Client: Co-Create Change


Background

Co-Create Change is a student run non-profit organization from UC San Diego with the purpose of helping the Myanmar community. They focus on Myanmar education and want to promote socially just economic development in the community. Our team got the chance to partner with them as they aim to address educational inequalities by promoting local entrepreneurship. To accomplish this, Co-Create Change needed a digital learning platform to serve as a communication and collaboration tool used to teach technical and business skills, and facilitate entrepreneurial ventures.
